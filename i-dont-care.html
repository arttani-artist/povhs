Untitled

By Anonymous

I don’t care.
Stop telling me to do well in school.
What’s the point?
I’m stuck in the same routines every day
Nothing is different
My life is filled with homework.
I don’t do anything fun.
Why do I have so much homework?
Why do I constantly get pressured?
Why is this happening to me.
You just expect everything from me.
Just STOP!!
Let me live my life for once
I don’t want to work at a desk.
I don’t want to work a 9 to 5.
I want to be free.
I want to sing to the world.
And write my heart out
And explore the world.
Not be stuck doing notes all day.
You have so many expectations for me.
But I ask
What’s the point of caring about anything?
If you’re just going to end up working in an office all day?
And not having any creativity?
I believe that caring about life means doing the things you love.
Not by doing a boring office job.
