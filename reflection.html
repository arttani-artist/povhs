Reflection

By Anon

When I look in the mirror,
what am I supposed to see?
A girl
brimming with knowledge
pumping joy through her veins
A girl
sweet and silent
yet loud
A personality enough to take up the room
A smile that prompts others to do the same

carrying the weight of a world
visible to no one at all,
sometimes not even to the soul
floating behind these muddled eyes
forever trapped in this accursed reflection of mine.
